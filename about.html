Who is Seijin?

Seijin-chan (星人ちゃん, English: "Ms. Alien") is an avatar made by Adhinka and is used as her internet identity, especially within the art community. Seijin is portrayed as an alien-like character and is often drawn in Japanese chibi-style. Under this name, she posts some of her artworks mainly through Instagram and Discord. She is also open for custom art commissions, both domestic (Indonesia) and international orders. During her free time, Seijin loves to draw characters from TV shows that she likes or her original characters.

Seijin's art style is mainly inspired by manga (Japanese-styled cartoon) with a touch of pop webtoon (web-based comics) for coloring. She developed the style herself, by self-learning how to draw traditionally since the 2nd grade of elementary school by imitating comic characters such as Conan from Case Closed (Detective Conan) and Naruto.

Early Design Prototype

Seijin's early design basically has a more dark and gloomy vibes, due to the color palette that was chosen came from one of her favorite colors which is eigengrau. Since Seijin means 'alien', the character has antennae as her special feature. In this prototype, her hair was drewn short with bangs covering her eyes, and she wore a simple black or eigengrau-colored shirt.

The design looked well (although people failed to tell that Seijin is a girl), but somehow the theme and design did not match quiet well. Despite the meaning of her name (alien), she decided to pick her name since Seijin itself comes from 2 chinese characters which represents star (星) and person (人). She evaluated that the combination of the 2 chinese characters should symbolically represents something more cheerful. Therefore, she changed the design to a more cheerful one with a combination of falun red, black, and grey.

Parody to Reality

Seijin actually has a genderbend alter called Seijin-kun (sometimes 'Seijoe'), which is a male version of her. The idea comes from an inside joke within one of the Discord art communities she belonged to. Seijin tested an AI chatbot in the internet along with her friends. She typed her name to see if the bot understood, but due to a bug, the bot answered by 'Hello, Joe' instead of 'Hello, Seijin'.

One of her friends said 'Seijoe' as a pun for 'Seijin + Joe', and it was widely spreading. From that day, she decided to create an alternative form of Seijin-chan. The official name of the genderbend version is 'Seijin-kun' as opposed to 'Seijin-chan' (-kun is a Japanese suffix for male, while -chan is for female), but some people who are familiar with the joke often use 'Seijoe' instead
