Hello, this is Adhinka!

ADHINKA Zahra Nur Annisa (student ID: 11420152) is a second year 3rd semester Ritsumeikan Asia Pacific University student from Indonesia. She is in the college of Asia Pacific Studies (APS) and is majoring in Culture, Society, and Media (CSM). She has a deep interest in technology and media, and that's why she is focusing on media studies in CSM. Previously, she had taken a short, 4-meeting course on web design held by a local community in her city. After finishing the course, she developed a greater curiosity on front-end engineering. She also joined a circle called APU Tech Club and is currently an active member.

Outside the university, Adhinka is often doing creative visual works, which are video editing and digital arts. She is currently a video editor in one of Indonesia's most popular YouTube channel called Nihongo Mantappu which has almost 9 million subscribers. Aside from that, she also loves to surf in the art community under the pen-name called Seijin.

Seijin-chan (星人ちゃん)

Seijin-chan (星人ちゃん, English: "Ms. Alien") is an avatar made by Adhinka and is used as her internet identity, especially within the art community. Seijin is portrayed as an alien-like character and is often drawn in Japanese chibi-style. Under this name, she posts some of her artworks mainly through Instagram and Discord. She is also open for custom art commissions, both domestic (Indonesia) and international orders. Check out more about Seijin's work and background story in About Seijin and visit her Instagram page for more info!
